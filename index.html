Round 1: Vs Jund

This Matchup felt pretty bad; they have enough interaction with our creatures that we have a hard time getting anything to stick. I also punted all of my Faithless Lootings which made it even harder

Lost 0-1
Round 2: Vs Devoted Company

This matchup feels good. Your interaction should be enough to break up the combo and the only worry is that they can just play a bunch of 2/2s and kill you with them. Seasoned Pyromancer is pretty good here giving both card selection and creatures to trade off.

Won 1-1
Round 3: Vs Burn

If you can connect with a Lightning Skelemental and make them discard 2 it feels like you should win. Brutality is great here. Bringing in Engineered Explosives might have been good here too but we have a lot of creature interaction already.

Sideboard:
In: Collective Brutality, Terminate
Out: 2x Ball Lightning
Won 2-1
Round 4: Vs Infect

Felt similar to Burn in that connecting with Lightning Skelemental wins it. The matchup is faster but less interactive on their end. I felt like I didn’t have time to mess around with Awakener reanimating since once they’ve lost their critical mass of cards you should be able to answer them 1 for 1 and win on your timeline. Side in all the interaction.

In: Engineered Explosives,Collective Brutality, 3x Fulminator Mage, 2x Plague Engineer, Terminate
Out: 4x Ball Lightning, 4x Thunderkin Awakener
Won 3-1
Faithless Looting is a hard card to play correctly. I misplayed the first 10 I cast. The Unclaimed Territory is a stand in for Cavern of Souls but it should really just be a land that taps for R/B or maybe another islet.
8-2

No deck changes.

Round 1: Vs 8Rack

The combination of Waste Not effectively shutting off Seasoned Pyromancer and Faithless Looting hurts here and retracing Raven's Crime means they have better top deck potential than we do.

Sideboard:
In: Engineered Explosives, Shenanigans, Collective Brutality
Out: 2x Fatal Push, Dreadbore

I didn’t realize that Brutality only got Instant and Sorceries so it’s probably not good. I wanted another explosives here and maybe leyline for Raven’s crime would be better than the other fatal push. Bringing in one more artifact removal spell might be good here as well since they run bridges. Dreadbore would stay in if they showed planeswalkers.

Lost 0-1
Round: 2 Vs Grixis Death Shadow

This is a tight matchup, on the one hand they do most of our work for us, on the other, they have a lot of interaction. We’re particularly weak to Gurmag. Another Engineered Explosives would have been nice to sideboard in.

Sideboard
In: Engineered Explosives , Shenanigans , Terminate
Out: Seasoned Pyromancer , Collective Brutality , Thunderkin Awakener

In hindsight Season Pyromancer should have stayed in, since it’s good for blocking and digging for answers. Shenanigans wasn’t needed (They’ll either have Leyline of the Void or an artifact they sac for graveyard interaction).

Won 1-1
Round: 3 Vs Titanshift

This feels like a pretty bad matchup. They’re pretty fast and our creature removal is pretty dead game one. They side in baloths to make our discard bad and cages to blank our recursion.

Sideboard
In: 3x Fulminator Mage , Collective Brutality , Shenanigans
Out: Dreadbore , 3x Fatal Push , Dreadhorde Arcanist

An extra Engineered Explosives would have been good here. Collective Brutality is a wasted card to bring in, it would be better as a By Force. I could see having Blood Moons would be nice too.

Lost 1-2
Round: 4 Vs Jeskai Ascendancy

Early disruption into connecting with a Lightning Skelemental makes this matchup pretty good.

Sideboard
In: Engineered Explosives , Shenanigans , Terminate , 2x Plague Engineer
Out: 4x Ball Lightning , Dreadbore
Wrapup: Overall, Dreadbore felt a little weak but I never ran into a deck with planeswalkers (which there were in the room). Finding room for another E.E. in the sideboard would be good and replacing the Unclaimed Territory with something else.
